Descreption:
RIYADTECH is a mobile application developed using Flutter to provide a centralized digital platform that supports the innovation ecosystem in Saudi Arabia. The idea was born from the need to connect fragmented stakeholders, such as startups, investors, and mentors, in a single space that fosters collaboration and opportunity.

The team analyzed market gaps, such as weak funding access and lack of post-accelerator support, and designed a user-friendly and scalable solution aligned with Vision 2030 goals.
